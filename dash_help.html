What is the EDSA dashboard?

The EDSA dashboard visualises the results of EDSA’s research into data science skills in Europe, and allows you to investigate the data yourself.

The results will help us to assess the demand for data science training in Europe and create new learning materials to help keep Europe ahead in this crucial domain.

The results of the research will directly influence the training and curriculum we create!

We want to collect data from every European country and every sector to do this.

Can you help us? ‘Click to contribute’ to add your own data.

What is the dashboard showing me?

Click on a country to reveal the aggregated and anonymised results of the data collected from focus groups, one-to-one interviews and data from the online survey. Countries in red, show areas where data has been collected.

EDSA dashboard overview

 

Select the ‘Capability’ box to reveal which data science skills those surveyed have the greatest capability in.

Selecting the capability box.

 

Take a look at the EDSA trend tracker at the bottom of the dashboard. It collects over 2000 data points each day from LinkedIn. We’re searching for key data science terms in advertised job profiles so that we can see the most advertised data science skills.

I want to take part! How can I contribute data?

Contributing data to the dashboard couldn’t be easier.
First select your country from the map and then select ‘Click to contribute’
The survey is interactive, and will only take 15 minutes to complete.
All data is anonymised and aggregated before being published to the dashboard.

 

I want to use the data. Can I access it?

Yes! The aggregated, anonymised data can be accessed from the ‘Get the data’ link at the bottom right hand corner of the dashboard. The data is licensed under a Creative Commons, Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC BY-SA 4.0) licence.
